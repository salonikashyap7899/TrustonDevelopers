Frequently Asked
Your Questions, Answered
Are the plots freehold or leasehold?
The majority of our portfolio is freehold — full and unconditional ownership rights transferred at registration. Any leasehold arrangements are disclosed upfront, before discussion of pricing.
Can I visit the site before deciding?
Absolutely. We arrange accompanied visits with all survey documents, boundary markings, and location context. You'll see exactly what you're buying — every corner, every boundary, every access point.
Is home loan or plot loan available?
Yes. We work with banking partners offering dedicated plot loans at competitive interest rates. Our team can guide you through the application and eligibility process at no extra cost.
How quickly is ownership transferred after payment?
Once documentation and payment are in order, registry through the sub-registrar is typically completed within 15 to 30 working days. We handle all coordination and accompany you on registry day.
What is the Jila Panchayat approval?
It is the local government's official sanction for the colony layout — confirming the land use, boundaries, and infrastructure plan are legally compliant and approved for residential development.
Can I hold the plot without building immediately?
Yes. Many of our buyers purchase for pure land appreciation. There is no mandatory construction timeline. You can hold, build, or sell the plot — full flexibility on your terms.
